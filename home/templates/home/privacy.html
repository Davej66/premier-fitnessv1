Your Privacy

Your Information

When you sign up for an account at FreelanceMeetups, we collect your personal information only that is necessary to identify you as a certain demographic, such as Name, Location, Industry, Role. We do this to allow you and other users to discover other users which have similar priorities and interests in meetups etc.


We collect your email address to allow us to contact you for account related information, and allow other users that you have connected to, to send you a message through the platform. Your email is not visible to other users when they send you a message, and will only be visible to them if you choose to respond to their email directly.

Cookies & Session Tracking

Currently, we do not use any form of analytics or tracking data in order to deliver the services on this site. There are however a few Cookies present that are essential to run the website.


The Cookies that are present do not identify you as a user, but simply register your browser use with Stripe, our third party payment provider, as well as add security tokens to a session.


In addition to those cookies, when you login to the site, a browser 'session' is created. This allows us to store minimal information in the browser when you use the site, that ensures we can access this information across multiple pages. An example of this would be your package selection - when you select an option, we store this in the session enabling us to carry this over into the remainder of the checkout pages. This session data is removed once the browser is closed and the session ends.
